Core concepts

How AI learns

Three main paradigms power modern AI systems.

Supervised learning
Train on labeled examples. Model learns to map inputs to correct outputs.
Unsupervised learning
Find hidden patterns in unlabeled data — clustering and anomaly detection.
Reinforcement learning
Agent earns rewards by taking actions in an environment. Trial and error at scale.
Large language models
Predict the next token. Trained on vast text, developing broad reasoning.
