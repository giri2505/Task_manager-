3 / 6 Core concepts

How AI learns

Three main paradigms power modern AI systems.

Supervised learning
Train on labeled examples. The model learns to map inputs to correct outputs.
Unsupervised learning
Find hidden patterns in unlabeled data — clustering, compression, anomaly detection.
Reinforcement learning
An agent earns rewards by taking actions in an environment. Trial and error at scale.
Large language models
Predict the next token. Trained on vast text, they develop broad reasoning abilities.

6 / 6 Future

What's next for AI

The next decade will be defined by agents, reasoning, and AI that acts — not just answers.

Agentic AI
Systems that plan, use tools, and complete multi-step tasks autonomously.
Multimodal models
Seamlessly understanding text, images, audio, video, and code together.
Scientific discovery
AI accelerating drug discovery, materials science, and climate research.
Personal AI
Models that know your context, adapt to you, and act on your behalf.
